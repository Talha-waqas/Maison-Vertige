Step By Step

How To Measure

  1. 01

    Place Foot on Paper

    Place a blank sheet of white paper on a flat, hard surface. Stand upright on it with your weight distributed evenly.

  2. 02

    Mark Length

    Holding a pencil vertically, draw a mark behind your heel and another mark at the tip of your longest toe.

  3. 03

    Measure Distance

    Use a ruler to measure the exact linear distance between the heel mark and the toe mark in centimeters.

  4. 04

    Compare with Chart

    Map the measured distance against the conversion chart above to determine your perfect Italian size.

Advice

Fit Notes

  • Our shoes run true to standard Italian (IT/EU) sizing. We recommend ordering your normal designer shoe size.
  • Between sizes? We highly recommend sizing up to the nearest half size for a more comfortable fit, especially for stiletto profiles.
  • For wider feet or higher insteps, consider our slide or mule models which provide relaxed lateral movement.
